About


The Fat Free Project's development team has over 55 years of combined coding and design experience.  More importantly, we've done a ridiculous amount of gaming.  We've witnessed catastrophic failures, transcendental successes, and everything in between.  In short, we've learned firsthand what works and what doesn't.


The staff of the Fat Free Project have always believed gaming is the most immersive way to tell a story.  Now we have stories of our own to tell and the ability to tell them.


Our approach to game design focuses on stripping away the elements of games that everyone hates until all that remains are the elements driving the story forward.  You won't find pointless or respawning enemies, cut scenes, escort mission, or endless grinding to get your level up.  Just satisfying, meaningful gaming and hopefully a few memories along the way.


In the coming year we will be designing and beta testing a new game.  For updates, follow us on Twitter, or sign up for our mailing list and we'll let you know when our official website and game blog goes live.
